Techniques

These are three simple techniques often used in various combinations. They can be adapted to suit individual preferences and needs. It's important to practice regularly for increased self-awareness, reduced stress, improved emotional well-being, and enhanced overall mental clarity.

Deep Breathing

Take slow and deep breaths, focusing on each inhale and exhale. Notice the sensation of your breath as you breathe in and out.

Body Scan

Close your eyes and gradually scan your body from head to toe. Notice any areas of tension or discomfort and allow them to relax.

Guided Visualization

Imagine a calm and peaceful place. Visualize yourself there, using your senses to explore the environment and relax your mind.
